Frequently Asked Questions

Are prices negotiable?

Yes, completely. We understand every project is different. Just let us know your requirements and we will build a custom package that matches your specific budget.

Will I have to pay monthly hosting fees for my web project?

No! For all frontend setups, we host your site on professional developer networks with built-in free subdomains and zero operational monthly costs.

How are in-game Minecraft builds delivered securely?

We join your target server manually to build out your custom base or terraform elements. For standalone worlds, we compile the directory or send clean schematics files directly.
